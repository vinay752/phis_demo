🎣 You’ve Been Phished!

Don't worry, this was just a security test. But in a real attack, your password would have been stolen just now.

How did you get caught?

  • The Sender: Did you check the "From" address? Real alerts come from @microsoft.com, not a random Gmail or Outlook address.
  • The Link: Hovering over the button would have shown it leads to a weird URL, not account.microsoft.com.
  • The Panic: Phishers use "Unusual Activity" or "Account Suspended" to make you act before you think.

Always check the URL before you type your password!
